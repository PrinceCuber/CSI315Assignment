Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I apply for admission?

You can apply online through your dashboard by clicking on "Apply for Admission". Complete the form and upload your supporting documents before the deadline.

2. What documents do I need to upload?

You will need to upload your BGCSE certificate or equivalent, national ID or passport, and any other relevant academic records.

3. Can I edit my application after submitting?

You can edit your personal information, but not your academic qualifications once the application is submitted. Please double-check before submitting.

4. What is the minimum point requirement for BSc General?

While it may vary slightly year to year, a minimum of 36 points from your top 6 subjects is generally competitive for BSc General.
